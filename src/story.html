Summary

The third technological revolution has not only greatly promoted changes in the economic, political and cultural fields of human society, but also influenced the way of life and thinking of human beings. With the continuous progress of science and technology, all aspects of daily life of human beings, such as clothing, food, housing, transportation and use, are also undergoing significant changes.

Characteristics

Science and technology play an increasingly important role in driving the development of productive forces, and the rate of transformation of science and technology into direct productivity has accelerated.

Results

Rapid development of microcomputers. The widespread use of electronic computers has promoted the automation of production, modernization of management, modernization of scientific and technological means.

Impacts

Continuous progress in production technology, continuous improvement in the quality and skills of workers, continuous improvement in the means of labor, and further increase in labor productivity.
